Chapter 05

Conclusion

Bringing it all together — what this journey through digital rights has taught us.

Summary of Learning

This portfolio looks at how freedom of expression and cybercrime law work together in the Philippines. It explains our right to free speech under the 1987 Philippine Constitution and the rules set by RA 10175. Through examples and activities, we saw how these two can sometimes conflict in real-life situations.

We learned that having the right to speak also means being responsible. We should express our thoughts honestly, respectfully, and be aware of how our words can affect others. We also realized that laws are important to protect people, but they should be used carefully so they do not limit our rights unfairly.

Rights & Law

Constitutional rights and cybercrime law must coexist in balance.

Digital Citizenship

Being online is a civic act — it demands responsibility and empathy.

Education

Digital literacy is the most powerful tool against online harm.

The era of technology makes your voice a valuable asset, as well as a great burden that you need to shoulder. Let your voice reach out to enlighten people, give hope, and question those in power. But wield it carefully, for every published word bears your personal accountability in both legal and public opinion perspectives.
